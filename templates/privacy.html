Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Wasabi Cloud Storage Bot ("we", "our", or "the service") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Telegram bot and web interface.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Telegram User ID: Your unique Telegram identifier
  • Username: Your Telegram username (if set)
  • Uploaded Files: Metadata about files you upload (filename, size, type)
  • Usage Data: Timestamps of uploads, downloads, and interactions
  • Technical Information: Device type, browser type, IP address (anonymized)

1.2 Files You Upload

Files you upload through the Telegram bot are:

  • Stored securely in Wasabi Cloud Storage
  • Encrypted during transmission and at rest
  • Accessible only via time-limited links
  • Never scanned for content except for virus/malware protection

Important Note

We do NOT collect or store:

  • Your personal messages beyond file metadata
  • Contact lists or Telegram chat history
  • Payment information (service is free)
  • Geolocation data beyond country-level for analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the storage service
  • To generate secure download links
  • To monitor service usage and performance
  • To prevent abuse and ensure security
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To improve service features and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Storage Locations

  • File content: Wasabi Cloud Storage servers (USA/EU regions)
  • Metadata: Secure database with encryption
  • Logs: Retained for 30 days for security monitoring

3.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end TLS encryption for all transfers
  • AES-256 encryption for files at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scans
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Automatic file deletion after link expiry

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With Wasabi Cloud Storage as necessary for file storage
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • During business transfers (merger, acquisition)

5. Data Retention

  • Uploaded Files: Stored until manually deleted or link expires (24 hours)
  • Metadata: Retained for 90 days after last activity
  • Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Inactive Accounts: Data purged after 1 year of inactivity

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request information about your stored data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your files and data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the service at any time
  • Data Portability: Request export of your data
To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@wasabi-bot.example.com

7. Third-Party Services

  • Telegram: Handles authentication and file transmission
  • Wasabi Cloud Storage: Stores your uploaded files
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (no personal data)

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via Telegram or the web interface. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
